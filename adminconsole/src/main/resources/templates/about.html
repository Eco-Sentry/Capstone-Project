What We Do

At EcoSentry, we specialize in developing a modifiable sensor device paired with a user-friendly interface that effortlessly display data over time through dynamic graphs and robust data collection features. Our innovative approach integrates an open-source code, allowing for customization and flexibility to various environmental monitoring needs. Whether it’s tracking air quality or temperature fluctuations, our sensor stations provide users with accurate and real-time data for proactive environmental management.
